About City Public School

At City Public School Noida, we espouse a philosophy that seamlessly integrates the timeless wisdom of traditional Indian cultural values with the progressive and rational perspectives of the Western world. Our mission is to foster a dynamic and inclusive learning environment that transcends the boundaries of caste, creed, and social status, thereby empowering children to become enlightened, compassionate, and globally conscious individuals.

City Public School Noida was founded in 2000 under the auspices of the esteemed Rama Devi Memorial Educational Society. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) up to the senior secondary level, boasting an impressive reputation for academic distinction and excellence. It proudly ranks among the top-rated CBSE schools in Noida and list of top schools in noida extension, known for its well-rounded development approach.

Situated on a sprawling four-acre campus, the school features state-of-the-art facilities, including a daycare and play school, catering to the diverse needs of its students. Recognised among the best nursery schools near me, City Public School offers a nurturing environment that lays a strong foundation for a child’s lifelong learning journey.

In addition to the school, the Rama Devi Memorial Educational Society has also established Rama Devi Kanya Mahavidyalaya, a pioneering college dedicated exclusively to women's education, offering specialized courses such as Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) and Basic Training Certificate (BTC) & B.A & B.Com

With its academic excellence and commitment to holistic growth, City Public School Noida is often featured in the List of schools in Noida recommended by both parents and educational platforms.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a nurturing and challenging educational environment where every student can achieve academic excellence, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into responsible, compassionate citizens. To impact value-based education to all irrespective of their caste, gender, and religion. We aim to make our students humane, kind, considerate, empathetic, ready to serve, and aware of their social responsibilities. To lead children from the darkness of ignorance to the light of knowledge and to bring out what is ingrained in them.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution recognized for inspiring students to become innovative thinkers, lifelong learners, and leaders who positively impact their communities and the world. To create an inclusive and supportive environment that challenges students to achieve academic excellence and personal growth while promoting values of respect, responsibility, and resilience. Ultimately, our vision is to be a beacon of educational excellence and a hub of creativity where students develop the skills, knowledge, and character necessary to thrive in a rapidly changing environment.
